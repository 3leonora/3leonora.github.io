Mathematical physics

Something to get you started:

  • "Lectures on the Geometric Anatomy of Theoretical Physics by Dr Frederic Schuller is a great introduction to applying mathematical concepts in physics. Covers basic differential geometry, Lie groups and their algebras, and representation theory.
  • These notes by Josh Kirklin, for the 2015 "Symmetries, Particles and Fields" Part III course taught by Nick Dorey.
  • David Tongs notes for literally anything, but my favourites are QFT, GR and String Theory.

Number theory + Physics:

  • Communications in Number Theory and Physics
  • Introduction to Modular Forms by Simon Rose
  • Modular Forms and Modular Congruences of the Partition Function, bachelor thesis by Shyam Narayanan
  • (Mock) Modular Forms in String Theory and Moonshine, notes by Miranda C. N. Cheng from Asian Winter School at OIST 2016
  • Modular forms in Quantum Field Theory, by Francis Brown and Oliver Schnetz
  • A Window into Zeta and Modular Physics, book based on lectures from MSRI workshops with the purpose of "bridge various gaps between mathematics and physics", aimed towards students
  • NAAP Working group
