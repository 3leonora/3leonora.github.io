Opportunities in physics/mathematics

Here I'll list opportunities for young students interested in physics/maths research. It is mainly aimed at Europeans. Last updated: 15th January 2023

  • Imperial Global Summer School
  • ESO Astronomy Camp, scholarships by your national astronomical association. I was elected 2016, read more here.
  • Maths Beyond Limits
  • Email researchers at universities and ask if they have a project for you. You're one email away to do something very cool!
